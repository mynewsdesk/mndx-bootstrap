Alerts

Alerts should always starts with a word that tell the user more about the feedback in short.

Wohooo! Thanks for signing up!
Morning! Looks like you are happy today!
Ooops! Your account will expire in two days!
Grrr! Don't drop coffee on my keyboard again!
<div class="alert alert-success" role="alert"><strong>Wohooo!</strong> Thanks for signing up!</div>
<div class="alert alert-info" role="alert"><strong>Morning!</strong> Looks like you are happy today!</div>
<div class="alert alert-warning" role="alert"><strong>Ooops!</strong> Your account will expire in two days!</div>
<div class="alert alert-danger" role="alert"><strong>Grrr!</strong> Don't drop coffee on my keyboard again!</div>
