Gutter

Those are the gutter variables that you can use. In the vertical flow we use steps of 30px, horizontal the rule is steps of 10px.

$gutter-horizontal = 10px
$gutter-horizontal-x2 = 20px
$gutter-horizontal-x3 = 30px
$gutter-vertical = 30px
$gutter-vertical-x2 = 60px
$gutter-vertical-x3 = 90px
$gutter-vertical-half = 15px
